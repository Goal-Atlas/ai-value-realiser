EVERSANA transforms this process with its end-to-end, AI-powered marketing agency platform built on Google Cloud. Previously, pharma marketing campaigns took weeks or months to develop, with traditional agencies executing tasks sequentially. Now, EVERSANA's specialized AI agents work simultaneously across business strategy, omnichannel planning, and content creation, integrating medical, legal, and regulatory compliance from day one. In just 30 minutes, pharma marketing teams can use EVERSANA's AI Agency platform to iterate from concept to approval-ready video ads, social media content, and compliant marketing materials.
