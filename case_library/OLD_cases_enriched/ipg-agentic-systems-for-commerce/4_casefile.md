# Case File: IPG's Agentic Systems for Commerce

## Executive Summary

Interpublic Group (IPG) has launched Agentic Systems for Commerce (ASC), an AI-powered unit that represents a strategic expansion beyond traditional marketing communications into quantifiable commerce solutions. The system leverages proprietary agentic AI and data from Intelligence Node to help brands optimize their e-commerce performance across digital channels.

ASC has demonstrated strong early traction with over 20 CPG brands participating in pilot programs, showing double-digit improvements in both impressions and sales. The initiative marks IPG's effort to create new revenue streams by productizing AI capabilities, moving from service delivery to solution products that deliver measurable business outcomes for clients.
