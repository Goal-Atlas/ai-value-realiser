# Klap AI Video Platform Case File

## Executive Summary

Klap, developed by Zigg co-founders Théo Champion and Victor Timsit, is an AI-powered video editing platform that transforms long-form content into short, vertical videos optimized for social media platforms. Launched in 2023, the platform has achieved remarkable scale with over 2 million users worldwide while maintaining operational efficiency with just a two-person technical team.

The platform leverages Google Cloud infrastructure to process petabytes of video data, using AI models to detect engaging moments in long-form content and automatically reframe footage from landscape to portrait format. Users can upload content and receive processed short-form videos within 10 minutes, dramatically reducing the traditional video editing workflow from hours to minutes.

Key achievements include maintaining 7% month-over-month growth, achieving 99.99% uptime, reducing infrastructure costs by two-thirds, and enabling a single engineer to manage infrastructure for 2 million users. The platform has facilitated the creation of 3.3 million clips for 1.7 million creators, positioning itself at the forefront of the rapidly growing AI video generation market.
