# Case File: Decathlon's AI-Driven Product Catalog Segmentation

## Executive Summary

Decathlon Austria partnered with SMEC to address significant challenges in their Google Ads Performance Max campaigns, which were hijacking brand search traffic and primarily promoting top sellers while neglecting strategically important products. The retailer needed better control over campaign optimization to align with business objectives and improve resource efficiency.

Through data-driven automation and intelligent product segmentation, SMEC helped Decathlon achieve remarkable improvements in their advertising performance. The collaboration resulted in a 22% increase in revenue and an impressive 52% boost in ROAS year-over-year, demonstrating the power of AI-driven campaign optimization in e-commerce marketing.
