# Microsoft Copilot Productivity Impact Case Study

## Executive Summary

This case study documents verified productivity improvements achieved by multiple organizations implementing Microsoft Copilot solutions, including Microsoft 365 Copilot and GitHub Copilot. The findings demonstrate significant time savings, quality improvements, and enhanced employee satisfaction across various business functions including software development, marketing, government services, and financial services.

Key highlights include dramatic time reductions in code development (75% reduction), presentation preparation (87.5% reduction), and marketing campaign deployment (67%+ reduction), alongside measurable improvements in work quality and employee engagement.

**C1: Work Quality Improvement**
- **Claim**: 52% of employees saw positive impact on work quality with Microsoft 365 Copilot
- **Status**: APPROVED
- **Evidence**: Direct measurement from Accenture/Avanade internal testing showing majority of employees experienced quality improvements
- **Impact**: Demonstrates measurable quality enhancement beyond just time savings

**C2: Cognitive Load Reduction**
- **Claim**: 31% of employees reported less cognitive fatigue using Copilot
- **Status**: APPROVED
- **Evidence**: Employee survey results from Accenture/Avanade pilot program
- **Impact**: Indicates AI assistance reduces mental strain and improves work experience
