# Case File: Stitch Fix's AI-Driven Personalization for Fashion Retail

## Executive Summary

Stitch Fix has built its entire business model around AI-driven personalization, fundamentally transforming how fashion retail operates by replacing traditional browsing and selection with algorithmic curation. The company leverages machine learning to analyze customer preferences, style data, and inventory to deliver personalized clothing selections at scale, creating a new category of retail experience.

The company's AI systems now drive 75% of styling decisions and have enabled significant business improvements including 40% increases in both average order value and repeat purchases, along with 15% improvement in customer retention. Beyond core styling, Stitch Fix has expanded AI applications to advertising copy generation, achieving 77% pass rates while reducing campaign creation time from two weeks to under one minute per asset.

However, recent financial performance shows mixed results, with the company achieving only 0.7% year-over-year revenue growth after several years of declines, suggesting challenges in scaling the AI-driven model despite operational improvements.
