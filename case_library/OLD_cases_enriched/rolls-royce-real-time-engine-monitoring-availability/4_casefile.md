# Case File: Rolls-Royce's Real-time Engine Monitoring and Predictive Maintenance

## Executive Summary

Rolls-Royce has implemented comprehensive real-time engine monitoring and predictive maintenance capabilities through their IntelligentEngine technology and service programs like TotalCare and CorporateCare. The company leverages automated data pipelines and digital twin technology to optimize maintenance operations, improve aircraft availability, and reduce operational disruptions.

The implementation demonstrates significant operational improvements, with over 97% of missed trips averted and average Aircraft on Ground (AOG) response resolution times reduced to under 24 hours. The CorporateCare program has achieved strong market adoption, covering almost 1,900 aircraft globally with over 70% of new Rolls-Royce powered aircraft deliveries enrolled in the program.

These AI-driven capabilities enable Rolls-Royce to transfer maintenance risk from aircraft owners to the OEM while providing predictable maintenance costs, reduced capital investment requirements, and increased aircraft residual value for operators.
