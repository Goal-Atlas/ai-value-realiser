# Case File: Airbus Skywise Aviation Data Platform

## Executive Summary

Airbus has transformed from a traditional aircraft manufacturer into a data platform provider through its Skywise initiative, launched in 2017 in collaboration with Palantir Technologies. The platform represents a fundamental shift in business model, connecting over 80 airlines worldwide to a cloud-based aviation data ecosystem that enables predictive maintenance, operational optimization, and safety enhancement.

The transformation demonstrates classic platform economics, where network effects increase value for all participants as more airlines share data. Skywise has evolved beyond operational efficiency to become a safety-critical tool, successfully identifying and preventing a potentially catastrophic A330neo accident through data analysis. The platform captures dramatically more data than traditional systems - up to 24,000 aircraft parameters compared to just 400 previously - enabling new service models and revenue streams for Airbus.
