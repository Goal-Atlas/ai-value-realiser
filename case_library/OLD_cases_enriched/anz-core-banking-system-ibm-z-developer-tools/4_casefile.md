# ANZ Core Banking System - IBM Z Developer Tools Implementation

## Executive Summary

Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Limited (ANZ), one of Australia's top-five banks, implemented a comprehensive mainframe modernization initiative using IBM zSystems technology to enhance their core banking infrastructure. The project focused on improving disaster recovery capabilities, backup performance, and overall system resilience to serve their extensive customer base of over 8.5 million users. ANZ's approach centered on building inter-site resiliency capacity through mirrored IBM zSystems servers, enabling faster disaster recovery and more timely mainframe backups while maintaining high availability standards required for critical banking operations.

## Detailed Findings

### Disaster Recovery and Backup Enhancement (C1)
**Status: APPROVED**

ANZ undertook a strategic initiative to enhance their mainframe backup capabilities and disaster recovery performance. The bank recognized the critical need for improved backup timing and faster recovery processes to maintain operational continuity for their banking services.

**Supporting Evidence:**
> "ANZ, a top-five bank in Australia, sought to increase its capacity for timelier mainframe backups and faster DR performance to ensure high availability for its more than 8.5 million customers."

**Analysis:** This finding demonstrates ANZ's proactive approach to infrastructure modernization, addressing the growing challenges of data volume increases and the need for more robust disaster recovery solutions in modern banking environments.

### Technical Implementation Approach (C2)
**Status: APPROVED**

ANZ implemented a sophisticated technical solution involving mirrored IBM zSystems servers to achieve their resiliency objectives. This approach enabled the bank to create redundant systems across multiple sites for enhanced disaster recovery capabilities.

**Supporting Evidence:**
> "They built out an inter-site resiliency capacity, running mirrored IBM zSystems servers"

**Analysis:** The mirrored zSystems architecture represents a modern approach to mainframe disaster recovery, moving beyond traditional backup solutions to provide real-time redundancy and faster failover capabilities.

### Customer Base and Service Requirements (C3)
**Status: APPROVED**

ANZ's modernization efforts were driven by the need to maintain high availability standards for their substantial customer base, highlighting the scale and criticality of their banking operations.

**Supporting Evidence:**
> "to ensure high availability for its more than 8.5 million customers"

**Analysis:** The customer base size underscores the importance of ANZ's infrastructure investments and the potential impact of any system downtime on both customers and the broader financial system.
