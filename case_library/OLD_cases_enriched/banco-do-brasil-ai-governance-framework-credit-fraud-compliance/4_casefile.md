# Banco do Brasil AI Governance Framework Case Study

## Executive Summary

Banco do Brasil, a 200-year-old Brazilian financial institution, has successfully implemented an AI-driven governance framework to enhance its credit fraud detection and compliance capabilities. The bank partnered with Napier AI to deploy advanced transaction screening technology, resulting in dramatic improvements in operational efficiency and regulatory compliance across its international operations.

Key achievements include a reduction in false positive rates to just 2% (representing a 90%+ improvement), enhanced processing capacity supporting up to 300,000 transactions annually, and significant operational cost savings through automated rule testing and calibration. The implementation demonstrates strong financial performance with a capital adequacy ratio of 15.2% and maintains 90.7% of transactions conducted electronically across its digital banking platform.
