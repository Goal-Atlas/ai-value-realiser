# SEON: Scaling Real-Time AI Fraud Prevention Platform

## Executive Summary

SEON, a Hungarian fraud prevention startup founded in 2017, has demonstrated exceptional growth by leveraging AWS cloud infrastructure to scale their real-time AI-powered fraud detection platform. The company achieved triple growth for three consecutive years, serving over 5,000 customers including major digital brands like Revolut, Plaid, and Spotify. Through strategic technology partnerships with AWS and ClickHouse, SEON has optimized their architecture to handle tens of millions of daily customer interactions while maintaining sub-3-second global response times. The company has raised $187 million in total funding, including an $80 million Series C round, and continues expanding globally with offices in Austin, London, Budapest, and Singapore.
