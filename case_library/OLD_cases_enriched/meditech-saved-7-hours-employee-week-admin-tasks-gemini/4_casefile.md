# MEDITECH: 7 Hours Per Employee Per Week Saved Through AI-Powered Administrative Task Automation

## Executive Summary

MEDITECH, a leading electronic health record (EHR) platform provider serving healthcare customers across 23+ countries, successfully implemented Google Workspace with Gemini to dramatically reduce administrative overhead. The company achieved an average of 7 hours saved per employee per week by automating manual tasks including note-taking, document drafting, and email revision. This implementation not only delivered quantifiable productivity gains but also improved job satisfaction by enabling employees to focus on strategic innovation rather than mundane administrative work.
