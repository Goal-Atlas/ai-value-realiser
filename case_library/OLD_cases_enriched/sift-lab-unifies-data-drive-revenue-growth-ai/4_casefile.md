# Sift Lab: AI-Powered MarTech Platform Case Study

## Executive Summary

Sift Lab, a Swedish AI company born from academic research at Umeå University's ICE Lab, has developed an AI-powered MarTech platform that helps businesses transform complex customer data into actionable marketing insights. Built on Google Cloud infrastructure, the platform combines predictive analytics, automated segmentation, and conversational AI to help marketers move beyond short-term campaign metrics and focus on long-term customer relationships.

The platform delivers significant performance improvements across multiple metrics, with campaign performance increases up to 200%, analytics workflows running 10x faster, and ROAS improvements of 40-50%. Enterprise clients report 5-7% annual revenue uplift and 15-30% increases in customer lifetime value for consented customers.

### Platform Capabilities

The case study highlights several key technical capabilities:
- **Conversational AI**: Sift Sense uses Vertex AI and Gemini for natural language understanding
- **Predictive Analytics**: Proprietary models for churn risk, lifetime value, and buying patterns
- **Integration**: Direct connectivity to Google Ads and marketing ecosystem
- **Segmentation**: Automated audience creation with intuitive naming via Gemini

### Client Examples

The case study provides a concrete example of a wellness products client where Sift Lab identified distinct customer personas (male bodybuilders vs. female vitamin shoppers), leading to more targeted marketing strategies that better served previously underaddressed female customers.
