# Case File: Ramp's AI-Powered Finance Automation Platform

## Executive Summary

Ramp has demonstrated exceptional growth in the finance automation space, achieving unicorn status through strategic AI implementation and aggressive scaling. The company reached a $22.5 billion valuation with $1 billion in annualized revenue by 2025, representing a dramatic increase from $13 billion valuation and $700 million revenue just months earlier. Key to this success has been Ramp's focus on AI-powered automation, which has enabled 3x productivity improvements for customers and significant operational efficiencies. The company has scaled to over 45,000 customers while maintaining strong unit economics, helping clients reduce expenses by 3.5% annually and accelerate month-end closing processes by 8 days.
