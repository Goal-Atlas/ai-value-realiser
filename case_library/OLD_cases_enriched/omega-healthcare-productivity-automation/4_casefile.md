# Omega Healthcare Productivity Automation Case File

## Executive Summary

Omega Healthcare, a global leader in revenue cycle management and healthcare services, has achieved significant operational improvements through its implementation of UiPath's AI-powered automation platform. Over a four-year period, the company has processed more than 60 million transactions using UiPath automations, resulting in substantial productivity gains and cost savings.

The implementation has delivered measurable results including a 100% increase in worker productivity, 40% reduction in documentation time, monthly savings of 6,700 worker hours, 50% reduction in turnaround time, and 99.5% process accuracy. The initiative has generated a 30% return on investment within the first year.

Omega Healthcare was recognized as a UiPath AI25 Award Winner at UiPath FORWARD 2024, following their previous recognition as an inaugural AI10 Award Winner, demonstrating their continued innovation in healthcare automation.

**Status**: Most claims approved with high confidence. One claim requiring additional review due to extraordinary nature of productivity improvement claims.
