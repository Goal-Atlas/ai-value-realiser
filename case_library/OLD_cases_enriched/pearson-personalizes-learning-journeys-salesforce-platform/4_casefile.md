# Pearson Education: Personalizing Learning Journeys with Salesforce Platform

## Executive Summary

Pearson Education has successfully transformed its customer service operations and learning journey personalization through comprehensive implementation of the Salesforce Platform, including Agentforce, Service Cloud, Sales Cloud, and Marketing Cloud. The education leader has achieved significant operational improvements, including 40% automation of routine customer inquiries through Agentforce and a 70% reduction in peak hiring needs during busy seasons. By consolidating disparate CRM systems onto Salesforce in 2015, Pearson has created unified customer experiences across its growing portfolio of educational brands while realizing substantial cost savings and efficiency gains.

## Detailed Findings

### AI-Powered Customer Service Automation

**Agentforce Impact on Service Operations**
- Agentforce handles an estimated 40% of routine customer inquiries, enabling the service team to prioritize complex learner needs
- The AI agent provides instant automated support for routine tasks including answering FAQs, processing returns, tracking orders, generating invoices, and confirming prices
- Agentforce accesses Pearson's knowledge library through Data 360, performing semantic searches to understand query intent rather than just matching keywords

**Chatbot Deflection and Efficiency**
- Achieved 50% deflection of inbound requests using chatbots, freeing agents to provide more personalized service for complex customer cases
- Improved average handle time for customer service interactions, with further acceleration through Einstein Bots implementation in 2020
- Enabled faster issue resolution through self-service capabilities and improved key metrics like first call resolution

### Operational Efficiency and Cost Savings

**Staffing and Resource Optimization**
- Reduced peak hire volume during the busy back-to-school season by 70% through Service Cloud implementation
- Realized cost savings by switching to an integrated cloud-based telephony provider
- Increased productivity for employees and agents while enhancing overall customer experience

**System Integration and Data Unification**
- Consolidated CRM footprint from disparate systems onto Salesforce in 2015 to improve efficiency and reduce costs
- Created 360-degree customer view enabling more personalized experiences across Pearson's growing portfolio of brands
- Unified customer and course data previously siloed across acquisitions like Credly, Mondly, and eDynamic Learning

### Technology Platform Capabilities

**Comprehensive Salesforce Implementation**
- Utilizes full Salesforce Platform including Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud, and Service Cloud
- Deployed integrated support community and interactive chatbots for enhanced self-service
- Leverages reports and dashboards to share insights with product management for continuous improvement

**Future AI Agent Development**
- Launched sales development agent for lead prospecting and qualification
- Planning marketing agents and new service agents for Virtual School enrollment
- Developing omni-channel experiences with Agentforce Voice capabilities
