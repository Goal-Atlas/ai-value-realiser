# Case File: Ocado's AI-Powered Grocery Fulfillment Platform Licensing

## Executive Summary

Ocado has transformed from a UK online grocery retailer into a global technology platform provider through its Ocado Smart Platform (OSP). The company now licenses its AI-powered fulfillment technology to 13 retailers worldwide, including major players like Kroger in the USA and AEON in Japan. This represents a fundamental business model transformation from direct retail operations to technology platform licensing.

The OSP combines artificial intelligence, robotics, and automation technology to manage everything from ecommerce to fulfillment, logistics, and supply chains. Ocado's platform demonstrates massive operational scale, with their latest warehouse capable of processing over 200,000 orders per week, making it the world's largest facility for online grocery. The platform has enabled client success stories like Bon Preu in Spain, which became the leading online grocery proposition in Catalonia and was recognized as the highest-rated online grocery service in Spain by OCU in 2024.
