# Case File: Air France-KLM's Digital Content Management and Infrastructure Optimization

## Executive Summary

Air France-KLM has implemented multiple technology initiatives to optimize their digital content management and IT infrastructure operations. The airline partnered with Contentstack to modernize their digital experience platform, enabling more agile content delivery across global touchpoints and markets. This composable architecture approach allows the company to maintain consistent content while adapting quickly to changing customer needs.

The most significant quantified impact comes from their migration to Atlassian Cloud Premium, which generated $600,000 in annual savings while eliminating downtime and maintenance costs. Additionally, the company has leveraged OpenText Documentum for over two decades to digitize aircraft maintenance documentation, replacing hundreds of thousands of paper documents with digital workflows that provide immediate access to critical maintenance information for 15,000 employees.
