# Case File: Sikshana Foundation - AI-Powered Educational Assistant

## Executive Summary

The Sikshana Foundation, in collaboration with Microsoft Research India, launched the Shiksha Co-pilot AI-powered digital assistant for teachers in Karnataka on October 18, 2024. This initiative aims to reduce lesson planning time while improving educational outcomes for government school teachers. The program initially targets 1,000 teachers across Karnataka with plans for statewide expansion.

The foundation has demonstrated significant scale in educational technology deployment, including assessment of 5.5 million students and digitization of 4,000 libraries. Their technology-driven approach spans multiple programs across Karnataka, Gujarat, and Andhra Pradesh, focusing on teacher empowerment and student learning enhancement.
