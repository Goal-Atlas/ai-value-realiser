# PwC Embeds AI into Consulting to Drive Efficiency and Client Innovation

## Executive Summary

PwC has undertaken a comprehensive AI transformation across its consulting operations, demonstrating significant measurable outcomes in cost reduction, operational efficiency, and client service delivery. The firm's strategic approach combines business transformation with AI implementation, resulting in $100 million in identified cost savings and substantial improvements in service delivery speed and accuracy.

Key achievements include the development of specialized AI tools like GL.ai for fraud detection, AI-powered translation services reducing external costs by 40%, and the creation of industry-specific AI solutions on AWS infrastructure. The transformation reflects broader industry trends, with 88% of executives planning to increase AI budgets and two-thirds of AI-adopting organizations seeing measurable productivity gains.

PwC's approach emphasizes a "people-first" methodology, ensuring employee upskilling while automating manual processes. The firm has established dedicated AI units and centers of excellence, positioning itself as a leader in AI-enabled consulting services across multiple sectors including healthcare, finance, and sustainability.
