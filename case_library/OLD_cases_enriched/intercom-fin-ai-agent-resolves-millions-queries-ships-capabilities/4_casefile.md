# Case File: Intercom Fin AI Agent Performance Claims

## Executive Summary

This case file examines performance claims made by Intercom regarding their Fin AI Agent across multiple product iterations (Fin 1, Fin 2, and Fin 3). The analysis covers 11 specific claims related to query resolution volumes, accuracy rates, efficiency improvements, and customer impact metrics spanning from the initial launch in March 2023 through 2025.

**Key Findings:**
- **10 of 11 claims approved** with strong supporting evidence
- **1 claim flagged for review** due to vague quantification
- Claims show consistent progression in AI agent capabilities and performance metrics
- Strong documentation across multiple official sources and third-party coverage

The claims demonstrate Intercom's AI agent evolution from handling 8 million queries initially to 14 million requests, with resolution rates improving from 25% to 66% across product iterations.
