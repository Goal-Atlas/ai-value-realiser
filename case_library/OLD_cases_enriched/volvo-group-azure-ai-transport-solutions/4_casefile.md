# Volvo Group AI-Driven Transport Solutions Case File

## Executive Summary

Volvo Group has established itself as a leader in AI-driven transport solutions, leveraging connected vehicle technology and data analytics to transform the trucking industry. With over one million connected trucks on the road worldwide, Volvo has achieved significant scale in delivering comprehensive digital services that optimize operations, reduce costs, and improve safety.

The company's approach centers on two key service areas: Uptime services that monitor vehicles remotely and predict maintenance needs, and Productivity services that enhance fleet management and driver efficiency. Through advanced AI and analytics, Volvo analyzes large volumes of data to accelerate product development and create innovative solutions that benefit customers while making transportation more sustainable and safer.

Volvo's commitment to AI innovation is demonstrated through internal hackathons that produce working prototypes with clear business value, and strategic investments in AI-driven companies like aifleet, which achieves 40% higher driver utilization than industry averages. With approximately 134,000 trucks delivered globally in 2024 and SEK 553 billion in net sales in 2023, Volvo Group continues to drive transformation in the transportation industry through AI and connected technologies.
