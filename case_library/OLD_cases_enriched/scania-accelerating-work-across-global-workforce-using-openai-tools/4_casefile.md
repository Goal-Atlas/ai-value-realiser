# Scania: Accelerating Work Across Global Workforce Using OpenAI Tools

## Executive Summary

Scania, the Swedish commercial vehicle manufacturer, has implemented a comprehensive AI transformation strategy through a direct partnership with OpenAI, deploying ChatGPT Enterprise across their global workforce. The initiative represents both a technological upgrade and a fundamental reimagining of work processes, positioning Scania as an early adopter in the sustainable transport industry.

The implementation spans from enterprise-wide ChatGPT deployment to innovative development practices like "Mob AI" programming, where teams collaborate with AI as an active participant. Scania has also developed specialized AI agents for developer experience optimization and is conducting scientific research with Stockholm School of Economics to measure productivity impacts.

Key outcomes include rapid skill development for engineers, automated developer feedback collection at scale, and significant time savings through process automation. The company views AI adoption through a value-driven lens, implementing AI tools where they provide genuine benefits to customers, employees, or society.
