# PepsiCo Computer Vision Shelf Inventory Optimization Case File

## Executive Summary

PepsiCo has implemented comprehensive AI and computer vision solutions to revolutionize its retail operations and supply chain management. The company deployed a Shelf Intelligence solution using edge AI and computer vision to automate merchandising processes, while also launching the Pepviz platform to provide retailers with data-driven insights for optimizing product placement and sales.

Key achievements include significant operational improvements in both retail merchandising and warehouse operations. The Shelf Intelligence solution enables real-time shelf auditing and merchandiser guidance, while data-driven retail optimization has delivered measurable sales increases for partner retailers. Additionally, warehouse automation initiatives have achieved substantial cost reductions and efficiency gains through AI-powered inventory management and robotic systems.
