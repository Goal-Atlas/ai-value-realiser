# Case File: WPP's AI-Powered Marketing Platform and Business Model Transformation

## Executive Summary

WPP has undertaken a fundamental business model transformation, investing over $300 million to build WPP Open, an AI-powered marketing platform that serves as the foundation of its entire business model. The company has shifted from traditional agency services to a platform-based approach, expanding its total addressable market to include smaller businesses that previously couldn't afford full-service agency support.

The transformation is evidenced by WPP's adoption of tech company operational practices, including 30 platform updates per day and 27,000 active monthly users. Through strategic acquisitions like InfoSum, WPP has positioned itself as the first ad-holding group to offer independent AI-powered marketing services, fundamentally changing how marketing services are delivered and consumed.
