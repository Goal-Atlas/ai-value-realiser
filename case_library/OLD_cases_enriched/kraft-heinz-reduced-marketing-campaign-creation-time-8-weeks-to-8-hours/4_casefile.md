# Kraft Heinz AI-Powered Marketing Content Creation Case

## Executive Summary

Kraft Heinz successfully implemented TasteMaker, a generative AI-powered marketing platform built on Google Cloud infrastructure, to dramatically accelerate their marketing content creation processes. The initiative achieved a remarkable reduction in new product content development time from 8 weeks to 8 hours, representing an 8x improvement in speed-to-market. The platform, which leverages BigQuery, Vertex AI, Gemini, Imagen, and Veo technologies, has achieved a 70% adoption rate among product development and marketing users while delivering 75% faster time to value for AI-supported processes.

The TasteMaker platform serves as a secure, prompt-less, web-based AI solution that integrates Kraft Heinz's proprietary brand intelligence with advanced AI capabilities to generate high-quality, on-brand content at scale. This transformation has enabled the company to maintain brand consistency while dramatically reducing the time and resources required for marketing content creation across their portfolio of over 200 brands.
