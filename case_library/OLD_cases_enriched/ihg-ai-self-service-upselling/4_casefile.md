# Case File: IHG Hotels & Resorts' AI-Powered Travel Planning and Guest Services

## Executive Summary

IHG Hotels & Resorts has implemented a comprehensive AI-driven strategy to enhance guest experiences and operational efficiency across its global portfolio of 6,000+ hotels. The company's initiatives include launching a generative AI-powered travel planner in partnership with Google Cloud, modernizing contact center operations with Genesys Cloud, and rebuilding its mobile app with advanced personalization features.

The results demonstrate significant business impact, with the redesigned IHG One Rewards mobile app achieving over 20% growth in revenue, usage, and downloads compared to pre-pandemic metrics. The app has become IHG's fastest-growing booking channel while maintaining a 4.9-star rating in the iOS app store. These technological investments, totaling over $300 million in recent years, are driving both operational efficiencies and enhanced guest satisfaction across the hospitality giant's operations.
