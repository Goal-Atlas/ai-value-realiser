# Case File: Endex - OpenAI Financial Model Reconciliation

## Executive Summary

Endex, a New York-based fintech startup founded in 2022, has developed an AI-powered financial intelligence platform that integrates directly with Excel to automate complex financial modeling and analysis workflows. By leveraging OpenAI's reasoning models (o1 and o3-mini), Endex has achieved significant performance improvements in financial data reconciliation, enabling finance professionals to focus on strategic decision-making rather than manual data processing tasks.

The platform addresses critical pain points in financial analysis by automating data gathering, reconciliation, and reporting while maintaining high standards of data security and compliance. Early adoption by hedge funds and financial institutions suggests strong market validation, with users comparing the experience to breakthrough AI moments.
