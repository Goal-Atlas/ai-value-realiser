# Crediclub Digital Transformation Case File

## Executive Summary

Crediclub, a Mexican financial institution, has undergone a significant digital transformation backed by substantial funding and strategic restructuring. The company raised over $80 million in funding led by L Catterton to accelerate its digital initiatives, resulting in impressive financial metrics and operational changes. The transformation included closing all 182 physical branches to transition to a fully digital platform, while achieving strong growth metrics including 40% annual growth rate and reaching 1.6 million customers by September 2024.

**Key Achievements:**
- Secured $80+ million in funding for digital transformation
- Achieved 515% capitalization ratio (well above 131% regulatory minimum)
- Successfully transitioned from branch-based to fully digital operations
- Reached 1.6 million customers and fifth-largest sector ranking
- Maintained 40% annual growth rate

**Areas Requiring Verification:**
- Total assets figure of MX$5.1 million appears inconsistent with company scale and requires verification
