# Providence AI Patient Message Routing Case File

## Executive Summary

Providence Health System has implemented multiple AI-powered solutions to address physician burnout and improve patient care efficiency, with a primary focus on intelligent message routing and triage. The centerpiece technology, **Provaria**, uses AI to categorize and prioritize MyChart patient messages, enabling urgent cases to be addressed within hours rather than days. Complementing this system are **Grace**, an AI chatbot that handles routine patient inquiries, and **MedPearl**, a clinical intelligence platform for specialty referrals.

**Key Verified Outcomes:**
- Critical mental health intervention delivered in <1 hour vs. previous 3-day delay
- 30% reduction in patient messages requiring physician attention through Grace chatbot
- Over 101,000 active monthly users on Providence app with Grace integration

**Areas Requiring Further Validation:**
- Specific quantification of "pajama time" reduction
- Exact metrics on inbox processing time improvements
- Measurable ROI figures for specialty referral optimization

## Detailed Findings

### **APPROVED CLAIMS**

#### C1: Emergency Response Time Improvement
**Claim:** AI-powered message routing enabled urgent mental health intervention within one hour instead of three days

**Status:** ✅ APPROVED

**Evidence:** Providence documented a specific case where their AI system tagged a depression-related message as urgent, leading to intervention within one hour. Previously, messages were processed in order received, potentially taking up to three days during high-volume periods.

**Impact:** Demonstrates life-saving potential of AI triage for mental health emergencies.

**Status:** ✅ APPROVED
