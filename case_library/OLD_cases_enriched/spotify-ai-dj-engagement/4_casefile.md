# Case File: Spotify's AI DJ Feature for Music Discovery and Engagement

## Executive Summary

Spotify's AI DJ represents a significant advancement in personalized music streaming, combining generative AI with voice cloning technology to create an interactive, conversational music discovery experience. Launched in 2023, the AI DJ feature has demonstrated remarkable success in driving user engagement, with listener engagement nearly doubling over the past year. The feature leverages Spotify's acquired Sonantic voice technology and OpenAI's generative AI to create real-time playlists accompanied by human-like commentary.

The AI DJ serves multiple strategic purposes within Spotify's ecosystem: enhancing user retention through personalized experiences, driving Premium subscription conversions, and creating a differentiated product offering. The feature now accepts voice requests in over 60 markets, allowing users to interact naturally with the AI to curate their listening sessions. This capability optimization has resulted in measurable improvements across key business metrics, including a 15% boost in user retention and significant contributions to Spotify's overall engagement and conversion funnel.
