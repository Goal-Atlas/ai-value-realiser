# Panasonic Energy & Palantir Battery Production Optimization Partnership

## Executive Summary

Palantir Technologies has entered into a multi-year partnership with Panasonic Energy of North America (PENA) to implement AI-driven operational analytics and smart technology at their EV battery manufacturing facilities. The partnership covers both the existing Gigafactory 1 facility in Sparks, Nevada (which supplies Tesla Motors) and the new facility in De Soto, Kansas, with implementation beginning in 2025.

The initiative aims to transform PENA's manufacturing operations through Palantir's Foundry platform, integrating edge sensors across factory lines, automating systems with AI, and providing real-time operational data to floor workers. The partnership specifically addresses challenges with disparate data systems and manual processes that have led to inaccurate data analysis, with expected outcomes including cost savings and reduced manufacturing waste.
