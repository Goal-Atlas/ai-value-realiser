# Case File: Klarna's AI Customer Service Automation

## Executive Summary

Klarna deployed an AI-powered customer service assistant that achieved remarkable operational efficiency gains within its first month of operation. The AI system handled 2.3 million customer conversations, representing two-thirds of Klarna's total customer service volume, while performing work equivalent to 700 full-time agents. This automation delivered significant improvements across key performance metrics including resolution time, accuracy, and customer satisfaction.

The implementation demonstrates substantial cost optimization potential, with Klarna estimating $40 million in profit improvement for 2024. Beyond customer service, the company has achieved 90% employee adoption of AI tools across departments, with particularly high usage rates in Communications (93%), Marketing (88%), and Legal (86%). The AI assistant operates 24/7 across 23 markets in over 35 languages, showcasing the scalability advantages of automated customer service solutions.
