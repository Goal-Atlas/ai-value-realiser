# ASOS AI Virtual Stylist and Customer Engagement Case Study

## Executive Summary

ASOS has implemented a comprehensive AI-driven personalization strategy across multiple touchpoints of the customer journey, including collaborative filtering for product recommendations, machine learning-powered fit assistance, and Azure OpenAI-powered conversational styling tools. While the company has deployed sophisticated AI technologies including virtual try-on, visual search, and predictive analytics, the specific performance metrics and quantifiable impacts of these implementations require further verification. Industry data suggests significant potential returns, with fashion segment pioneers achieving 3.2x ROI from generative AI adoption and smaller retailers experiencing 31% faster revenue growth when implementing AI solutions.

## Key Technologies Implemented

- **Collaborative Filtering**: Machine learning algorithms for product recommendations based on browsing patterns and customer similarity
- **Fit Assistant**: ML-powered size recommendation system analyzing past purchases, returns, and measurements
- **AI Stylist**: Azure OpenAI-powered conversational tools for personalized style advice
- **Profile Builder**: Preference learning system using 36 sample products for personalization
- **Supply Chain AI**: TrusTrace partnership for AI-powered supply chain mapping and traceability
- **Backend Automation**: Microsoft Copilot, Power Automate, and GitHub for operational efficiency
