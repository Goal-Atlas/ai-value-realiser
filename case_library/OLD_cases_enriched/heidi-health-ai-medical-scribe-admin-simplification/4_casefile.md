# Case File: Heidi Health AI Medical Scribe Administrative Simplification

## Executive Summary

Heidi Health's AI-powered medical scribe platform demonstrates significant impact on clinical administrative efficiency and patient care quality. Based on a comprehensive 25-day trial with 47 GPs across 2,800+ patient consultations, the platform achieved substantial reductions in documentation time (51% during appointments, 61% after-hours) while improving clinician-patient interactions and reducing stress. The company has achieved significant market penetration, serving 1 in 2 GPs across the UK and supporting over 1.5 million NHS appointments monthly, with a current valuation of $465 million.

**Key Verified Outcomes:**
- 51% reduction in documentation time during appointments
- 61% reduction in after-hours administrative work
- 78% of GPs reported better patient rapport and reduced cognitive load
- 58% reduction in documentation-related stress
- Strong market adoption with significant NHS integration
