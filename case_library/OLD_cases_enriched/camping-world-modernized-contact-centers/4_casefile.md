# Case File: Camping World - AI-Powered Contact Center Modernization

## Executive Summary

Camping World, the leading retailer of recreational vehicles, successfully implemented IBM Watson AI-powered virtual assistant "Arvee" to modernize their contact centers during the COVID-19 pandemic. Facing a surge in customer demand and strained call center operations, the company deployed conversational AI to provide 24/7 customer service, reduce wait times, and improve operational efficiency.

The implementation delivered significant measurable results: 40% increase in customer engagement, 33% improvement in agent efficiency, and 33-second reduction in wait times. The AI assistant enabled round-the-clock service coverage and comprehensive call data capture for the sales team, addressing critical gaps in after-hours customer support.
