# Case File: HELLENiQ ENERGY AI Productivity Transformation

## Executive Summary

HELLENiQ ENERGY, a leading integrated energy group in Southeast Europe, successfully implemented Microsoft 365 Copilot as part of a comprehensive digital transformation strategy. The implementation, supported by PwC Greece, resulted in significant productivity improvements and operational enhancements across the organization.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Over 70% of employees reported enhanced productivity
- Meeting catch-up times improved by 4x
- 71 tailored use cases developed across business functions
- Successful deployment of AI-powered HR assistant "HRaklis"
- Establishment of a community of 600 Digital Stars for adoption support

The transformation focused on three core pillars: continuous innovation, employee-centric design, and operational excellence, with a gradual and personalized onboarding approach that placed people at the center of the strategy.

## Implementation Details

The transformation was executed through a structured approach:

1. **Initial Rollout:** Microsoft 365 Copilot deployment with early access experimentation
2. **Use Case Development:** 71 tailored applications across Finance, Operations, Marketing, HR, and Customer Service
3. **Adoption Strategy:** Comprehensive playbook with workshops and personalized onboarding
4. **Support Structure:** 600 Digital Stars community and Digital Heroes support team
5. **Specialized Solutions:** AI-powered HR assistant "HRaklis" for internal service delivery
