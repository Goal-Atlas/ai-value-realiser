# Case File: Bitazza Thailand - Scaled Broker Platform for Crypto Adoption

## Executive Summary

Bitazza Thailand, a licensed digital asset broker operating under Thai SEC supervision, has successfully leveraged Google Cloud infrastructure and AI services to scale their cryptocurrency trading platform and drive adoption in Thailand's top-20 global crypto market. The company achieved remarkable operational improvements including supporting $4.6 billion in transactions in 2024, accelerating financial statement creation by 75% (from one year to three months), and maintaining regulatory compliance with near-real-time reporting capabilities.

The platform integrates multiple source exchanges through secure APIs, handles complex data including digital assets, fiat currencies, and third-party feeds, while serving over 80 cryptocurrency trading pairs. Bitazza has also expanded beyond traditional exchange services to include Web3 payment solutions, non-custodial wallets, and business-to-business services, positioning itself as a comprehensive gateway for crypto adoption in Southeast Asia.
