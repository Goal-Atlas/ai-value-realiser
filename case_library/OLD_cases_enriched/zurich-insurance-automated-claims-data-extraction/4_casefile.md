# Case File: Zurich Insurance Group - Automated Claims Data Extraction and Processing

## Executive Summary

Zurich Insurance Group has implemented a comprehensive AI and automation strategy across multiple business functions, with significant focus on claims processing, fraud detection, and customer service automation. The company has deployed over 160 AI use cases across different divisions, establishing an AI Assurance Framework (AIAF) in 2022 to govern AI deployment.

Key achievements include:
- **Operational Efficiency**: 55 digital workers managing 120+ processes, processing 3 million transactions
- **Claims Processing**: Reduced processing time from 24 hours to 3 working hours through chatbot implementation
- **Fraud Detection**: Substantial fraud prevention savings through AI-powered detection systems
- **Financial Operations**: 85% automation hit rate in cash application, freeing 33% of team resources
- **Customer Service**: 24/7 claims reporting capability with 60% of claims now submitted outside office hours

The implementation demonstrates measurable ROI through cost savings, resource optimization, and enhanced customer experience across multiple business units.
