# Lowe's AI-Powered Search and Inventory Management Case Study

## Executive Summary

Lowe's has implemented a comprehensive AI strategy focused on enhancing customer experience through advanced search capabilities and inventory management systems. The company has deployed multiple AI-powered tools including the ChatGPT-powered assistant "Mylow," Visual Scout for visual product discovery, and associate-facing digital companions. These initiatives have delivered significant business results, with Mylow doubling conversion rates and the company achieving a 26% sales increase from Q3 2018 to Q3 2024. Lowe's has also strengthened its position in the professional contractor market, growing this segment from 19% to 30% of total sales.

## Technology Architecture

Lowe's AI implementation leverages a comprehensive Google Cloud infrastructure including:
- **Vertex AI Vector Search**: Powers visual similarity matching for Visual Scout
- **Vertex AI Feature Store**: Manages product image embeddings
- **BigQuery**: Hosts enterprise-wide product metadata
- **Google Kubernetes Engine**: Deploys Visual Scout application
- **Dataproc**: Processes embeddings for new items
